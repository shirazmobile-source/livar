Design a CRM user interface with exactly this visual DNA:

- Minimal, professional, clean, fast, and free of clutter
- Overall structure should be card-based
- The only brand color should be `#FF5500`
- Main palette: white, black, and soft grays
- Generous but controlled radius: `14px` to `18px`
- Shadows should be soft and shallow
- Sticky topbar with blur and a modern iOS/macOS-like feel
- Navigation items should use a pill shape
- Primary button should be orange with black text and a very bold font weight
- Forms should be simple, clear, rounded, and use a subtle orange focus ring
- Badges and tabs should be pill-shaped with a very faint tint
- Tables should be clean, rounded, with neutral headers and very subtle hover states
- Desktop: multi-column KPI tiles, analytical cards, and modern tables
- Mobile: card stack, accordion, two-column KPI grid, and a sticky bottom action bar
- Dark mode: near-black background with semi-transparent panels and low-contrast borders
- Avoid heavy gradients, multiple colors, neon, heavy shadows, and decorative effects
- The result should convey a luxurious, fast, precise, and trustworthy CRM feel
